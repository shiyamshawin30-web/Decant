Returns & Refunds

We strive for absolute satisfaction with every discovery. Our return policy is designed to maintain the highest standards of hygiene and authenticity for our community.

1. Eligibility

Due to the nature of our products, returns are only accepted for items that remain in their original, unopened, and factory-sealed packaging within 7 days of delivery.

2. Quality Concerns

In the rare event of a manufacturing defect or damage during transit, please contact our concierge team via WhatsApp with photographic evidence within 24 hours of receipt.

3. Process

Approved refunds are processed via the original payment method or bank transfer within 5 business days of the item being returned and inspected.
