The DecantLAB Story

In the pursuit of olfactory perfection, DecantLAB was founded in 2026 as a sanctuary for the discerning fragrance connoisseur. Based in Wattala, our laboratory was born from a singular vision: to democratize luxury by providing absolute transparency and authenticity in every drop.

We believe that a fragrance is more than a scent—it is a liquid memory, a signature of identity. By sourcing directly from authorized distributors and implementing a rigorous multi-stage verification process, we ensure that every decant and bottle in our collection is a masterpiece of its original house.

Today, DecantLAB stands as Sri Lanka's premier destination for niche and luxury fragrances, offering an unparalleled concierge experience that helps you find your signature essence in an world of endless possibilities.
